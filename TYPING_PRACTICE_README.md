# Pinyin Typing Practice - Learning Guide

## Overview

The Pinyin Typing Practice is a stress-free learning tool designed to help you master Chinese pinyin through typing. Unlike the Typing Race game which focuses on speed, this practice mode emphasizes accuracy and learning at your own pace.

## How It Works

### Getting Started

1. **Select Categories**: Choose one or more vocabulary categories to practice:
   - Pronouns (我, 你, 他, etc.)
   - Family (爸爸, 妈妈, 儿子, etc.)
   - Numbers (一, 二, 三, etc.)
   - Time (今天, 明天, 小时, etc.)
   - Countries (中国, 美国, 日本, etc.)
   - Food (米饭, 面条, 水果, etc.)
   - Animals (狗, 猫, 鸟, etc.)
   - Verbs (吃, 喝, 去, etc.)
   - Other (miscellaneous vocabulary)
   - Names (common Chinese names)

2. **Choose Practice Mode**:
   - **Sequential**: Go through words in order, perfect for systematic learning
   - **Random**: Words appear randomly, with smart selection that prioritizes unseen words
   - **Review Mistakes**: Focus only on words you've previously struggled with

3. **Toggle Translation**: Decide whether to show English translations (recommended for beginners)

### During Practice

#### Main Display
- **Large Chinese Character**: The character you need to type the pinyin for
- **English Translation**: Optional helper showing the meaning (can be toggled on/off)
- **Input Field**: Where you type the pinyin

#### Typing Rules
- **With Tones**: Type exact pinyin with tone marks (e.g., "nǐ hǎo")
- **Without Tones**: Type pinyin without tones (e.g., "ni hao") - also accepted!
- **Apostrophes**: Words like "nǚ'ér" can be typed as "nu er" (with space)
- **Real-time Feedback**:
  - ✓ Green checkmark = you're typing correctly, keep going!
  - ✗ Red X = check your spelling

#### Learning Aids

1. **Hint Button** 💡
   - Shows the first half of the pinyin
   - Helps when you're stuck but want to figure it out
   - No penalty for using hints

2. **Show Answer Button**
   - Reveals the complete pinyin with tones
   - Also shows the English translation
   - After seeing the answer, you must type it correctly to continue
   - This reinforces learning through practice

3. **Next Word Button** →
   - Skip to the next word at any time
   - Useful when you want to move on without completing the current word

### Progress Tracking

#### Progress Bar
- **Sequential Mode**: Shows your position (e.g., 5/20 words)
- **Random Mode**: Shows unique words practiced (e.g., 12/20 unique words)
- **Review Mode**: Shows progress through your mistake list

#### Statistics Dashboard
- **Accuracy**: Percentage of words typed correctly on first try
  - 🟢 Green: 90%+ (Excellent!)
  - 🟡 Yellow: 70-89% (Good progress)
  - 🔴 Red: Below 70% (Keep practicing)
- **Words Learned**: Total unique words you've successfully typed
- **Current Streak** 🔥: Consecutive correct answers without mistakes
- **Best Streak** ⭐: Your record for consecutive correct answers

### Learning Flow

1. **See a Word**: A Chinese character appears
2. **Think**: Recall the pinyin pronunciation
3. **Type**: Enter the pinyin (with or without tones)
4. **Get Feedback**: 
   - Correct = "Correct! ✓" message + move to next word after 1.5 seconds
   - Incorrect = Option to get hint or see answer
5. **Learn from Mistakes**: When shown the answer, you must type it to reinforce learning

### Smart Features

#### Random Mode Intelligence
- Prioritizes showing words you haven't seen yet
- Once all words are seen, it randomizes from the full set
- Progress bar turns green when you've seen every word at least once

#### Mistake Learning
- When you use "Show Answer", you must type the correct pinyin
- This ensures you practice words you don't know
- The system tracks which words you struggle with for Review Mode

#### No Time Pressure
- Take as long as you need for each word
- Focus on accuracy and memory, not speed
- Perfect for beginners or detailed study sessions

### Tips for Effective Learning

1. **Start Small**: Begin with one category you're comfortable with
2. **Use Sequential Mode First**: Learn words in a structured order
3. **Keep Translation On**: When starting, seeing meanings helps create associations
4. **Don't Skip Too Much**: Try hints before showing answers
5. **Practice Mistakes**: Use Review Mode to focus on challenging words
6. **Build Streaks**: Try to maintain accuracy streaks to reinforce good habits
7. **Regular Practice**: Short daily sessions are better than long irregular ones

### Comparison with Typing Race

| Feature | Typing Practice | Typing Race |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| Time Limit | None | 1, 5, or 10 minutes |
| Pressure | Low - learn at your pace | High - beat the clock |
| Focus | Accuracy & learning | Speed & points |
| Mistakes | Must practice correct answer | Shows answer, moves on |
| Progress | Tracks coverage & mastery | Tracks WPM & score |
| Best For | Learning & memorization | Testing & gamification |

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Enter**: Give up and show answer (when stuck)
- **Tab**: Focus on input field
- Click any button or use mouse for all controls

### Progress Persistence

- Your progress is saved locally in your browser
- Tracks correct/incorrect attempts for each word
- Review Mode uses this data to show words you need to practice
- Progress carries over between sessions

## Goal

The ultimate goal is to help you recognize Chinese characters and correctly type their pinyin pronunciation. This builds a strong foundation for:
- Reading Chinese text
- Using pinyin input methods
- Understanding pronunciation
- Building vocabulary through repetition

Remember: There's no rush! Take your time, learn at your own pace, and celebrate small victories as you master each character.
